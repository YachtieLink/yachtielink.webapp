# Yachtielink - Phase History

---

## Phase 0 - Problem Formation (Complete)

**Objective:** Define why trust fails in yachting, which models are invalid, and the non-negotiables that prevent incentive collapse.

**Completed:** 2025-11-15

**Artifacts produced:**
- Problem definition and root cause analysis
- Constitutional decisions (D-001 through D-003)
- Non-goals documentation
- Core principles

**Key insight:** The industry doesn't lack information. It lacks a neutral place for identity and trust signals to accumulate without being corrupted by money or power.

---

## Phase 1A - Portable Profile + Yacht Graph + Endorsements (Current)

**Objective:** Ship the core wedge: crew-owned profile infrastructure that turns yacht-backed work history into a living colleague graph and endorsement loop.

**Started:** 2026-01-01  
**Target alpha:** ~3-4 months after build start

### What Phase 1A Ships

- Profile, employment history, certifications
- CV import
- Yacht entities and employment attachments
- Colleague graph from shared attachments
- Endorsement requests and endorsements
- Public profile page
- PDF snapshot
- Paid presentation upgrades

### Completion Envelope

Phase 1A is complete once all of the following are true:

1. Graph formation is healthy
2. Endorsement request/completion loop is working
3. Organic profile/PDF sharing proves standalone utility
4. Paid presentation upgrades show real willingness to pay

**Tripwires that pause growth:**
- Attachment creation is too low to form a useful graph
- Endorsement completion is too weak to validate the wedge
- Organic share rate is too low
- Repeat flag or dispute rate is too high
- Time-to-first-endorsement is too slow

---

## Phase 1B - Discovery + Availability (Planned)

**Objective:** Expand the crew-side product once the yacht graph is healthy without diluting trust.

**Planned features:**
- Availability toggle
- Limited crew search
- Expanded analytics and convenience upgrades

**Key constraint:** Discovery may improve workflow efficiency, but it may not alter trust outcomes.

---

## Phase 1C - Hiring + Recruiter Access (Planned)

**Objective:** Add adjacent commercial workflows only after the crew-side graph stands on its own.

**Planned features:**
- Peer hiring surfaces
- Recruiter access model
- Additional discovery tooling

**Key constraint:** Payment may buy efficiency, never influence, moderation power, or credibility outcomes.

---

## Phase 2 - Social + Industry Layers (Deferred)

**Objective:** Add later social surfaces and diagnostics only after the trust substrate is stable.

**Potential features:**
- Timeline / posts / interactions
- Messaging / contacts
- IRL connection system
- Yacht dashboards and later diagnostics

**Key constraint:** Any later layer must strengthen or safely sit on top of the graph, not distract from it.
