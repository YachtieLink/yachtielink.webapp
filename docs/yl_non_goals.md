# Yachtielink - Explicit Non-Goals

These exclusions are binding for the current build target unless explicitly noted.

---

## Not a recruiter product in the first build

**Why:** The first wedge is crew-side graph formation. Recruiter workflow can wait until the yacht graph and endorsement loop are real.

**What this prevents right now:**
- Recruiter dashboards
- Recruiter subscriptions or credit unlocks
- Recruiter-facing search as a launch surface
- Building around agency workflow before the crew graph is healthy

**What this allows later:**
- Read-only recruiter access once the crew-side product stands on its own
- Discovery tooling that monetises efficiency, not trust

**Note:** This is a sequencing constraint, not a permanent prohibition.

---

## Not an employer-pays hiring platform

**Why:** When employers pay for hiring, the platform optimises for employer satisfaction over crew truth.

**What this prevents:**
- Paid job postings or promoted listings
- Employer-only accounts
- Placement fees
- Any revenue derived from hiring transactions
- Algorithmic job matching that obscures graph relationships

**What this allows later:**
- Crew-side hiring flows if both sides are visible nodes in the graph
- Workflow tooling that uses the graph without selling influence

---

## Not a rating or review platform

**Why:** Star ratings and open reviews create coercion dynamics, legal exposure, and false precision.

**What this prevents:**
- Numeric scores
- Open review boxes
- Aggregated reputation scores
- Any UI that summarises someone as "highly rated" or "poorly rated"

**Note:** Endorsements are contextual, gated by shared employment, and never reduced to a number.

---

## No global discovery feed

**Why:** Global discovery mechanics incentivise engagement hacks and ranking pressure that corrupt signal integrity.

**What this prevents:**
- Public explore feeds
- Trending lists
- Viral sharing mechanics
- Algorithmic content surfacing

**Note:** Timeline is currently deferred. If it ships later, there is still no public discovery layer.

---

## Not reputation management software

**Why:** No tools will exist to delay, suppress, buy, rehabilitate, or optimise trust outcomes through payment.

**What this prevents:**
- Paid removal or suppression tooling
- Trust boosts
- Paid dispute priority
- Any feature that lets money influence how trustworthy someone appears

**Note:** Presentation polish is allowed. Trust outcomes are not.

---

## Not a discovery platform in the current build

**Why:** The current product wedge is graph formation, not browsing.

**What this prevents right now:**
- Public search or browse of all profiles
- Featured profile lists
- Activity-driven visibility
- Building discovery before the graph is healthy

**Note:** Profiles are visible via direct link. Search and discovery may be introduced later if they remain clearly decoupled from trust outcomes.

---

## Not a messaging platform

**Why:** The product is identity and trust, not communication.

**What this prevents right now:**
- Group chats
- Broadcast messaging
- Read receipts, typing indicators, presence
- Building engagement loops before the graph wedge is working

**Note:** Messaging is currently deferred.
